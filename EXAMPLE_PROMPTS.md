## üìù Prompt Formula

**Basic Structure:**
```
[Subject] + [Action/Scene] + [Environment] + [Lighting] + [Style/Mood]
```

**Example:**
```
Dragon flying over medieval castle at sunset with dramatic clouds, cinematic fantasy style
```

**Advanced Structure:**
```
[Camera Movement] + [Subject] + [Action] + [Environment] + [Time/Weather] + [Lighting] + [Technical Details] + [Style]
```

**Example:**
```
Slow dolly shot of a vintage car driving through rainy city streets at night with neon reflections, shallow depth of field, cinematic film noir style
```

## üéØ Model-Specific Tips

### For Realistic Models (Sora, Runway, Luma)
- Focus on natural scenes and realistic physics
- Mention camera techniques
- Include lighting details
- Specify realistic atmospheres

### For Artistic Models (Flux + SVD)
- Embrace creative and abstract concepts
- Try unique art styles
- Experiment with colors and patterns
- Don't be afraid to be imaginative

### For All Models
- Start simple, then add details
- Test variations of successful prompts
- Keep prompts under 200 characters for best results
- Be patient - quality takes time!
